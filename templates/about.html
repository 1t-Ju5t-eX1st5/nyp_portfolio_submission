My Story

I first learnt to code in Primary 3, when my Co-curricular Activity (CCA) teacher taught us how to use Scratch to develop a simple game. After that session, I developed an interest for computer science and programming, and wanted to keep improving my ability to code in order to develop better programs that can help others.

In Secondary 2, I participated in the Youth Cyber Exploration Programme (YCEP) that the Cyber Security Agency of Singapore (CSA) hosts annually. This competition provided me with an entirely new perspective on cybersecurity, offering a glimpse into the world of hacking and the exhilaration that accompanies engaging in CTF challenges. It was also through this event that led me to meet my current group of friends (Yes But No - YBN)
