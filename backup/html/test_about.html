My Story

I first learnt to code in Primary 3, when my Co-curricular Activity (CCA) teacher taught us how to use Scratch to develop a simple game. After that session, I developed an interest for computer science and programming, and wanted to keep improving my ability to code in order to develop better programs to help others.

In Secondary 1, I stumbled across the Youth Cyber Exploration Programme (YCEP) that the Cyber Security Agency of Singapore (CSA) hosts annually. Although I did not win anything from YCEP 2021, it gave me a taste of what hacking was like, as well as the joy and excitement that arises from participating in CTF challenges

My Accomplishments and Projects

- First place for Team CTF - Advanced Youth Cyber Exploration Programme (2021)
- Second runner-up - Youth Cyber Exploration Programme, Hack'n'Flag (2022)
- Participant - HacktheBox's Cyber Apocalypse 2023

My first coding project was a Discord bot (Le Shroom Boi), which initially started as an inside joke between a group of friends. However, the project was eventually abandoned due to the increasing amount of schoolwork. Afterwards, I started experimenting with other publically available APIs, and created a simple program to interact with the EVE Online API
